Perfect Steak Recipe

Steak Overview

Cooking the perfect steak is easy when you follow these simple steps. Start by choosing a good cut of steak, such as ribeye, filet mignon, or New York strip. Season the steak with salt, pepper, and any additional spices you like. Heat a cast-iron skillet or grill, then cook the steak to your desired level of doneness, whether it's rare, medium, or well-done.

For a richer flavor, you can add butter and garlic in the final minutes of cooking. Let the steak rest after cooking to allow the juices to redistribute. Slice and serve with your favorite sides for a delicious meal.

Ingredients

  • Steak (ribeye, filet mignon, or New York strip)
  • Salt
  • Pepper
  • Olive oil
  • Butter
  • Garlic (optional)
  • Fresh rosemary or thyme (optional)
  • Lemon (optional, for serving)

Steps

  1. Season the Steak: Generously season your steak with salt, pepper, and any other spices you prefer.
  2. Heat the Pan or Grill: Preheat your skillet or grill to medium-high heat. Add a bit of olive oil to prevent sticking.
  3. Cook the Steak: Place the steak on the hot skillet or grill and cook for about 3-5 minutes on each side, depending on thickness and desired doneness.
  4. Add Butter and Garlic: During the last minute of cooking, add butter, smashed garlic, and fresh herbs for extra flavor.
  5. Rest and Serve: Remove the steak from the heat and let it rest for 5-10 minutes before slicing. Serve with a lemon wedge or your favorite sides.
